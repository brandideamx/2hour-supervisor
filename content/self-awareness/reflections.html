What Now?
Stereotypes can have an impact on how we view other employees in our workplace. If we are not careful, our biased assumptions about our co-workers may affect workplace decisions such as:

Hiring:
  • Resume screening
  • Interviewing
  • Onboarding
  • Compensation
Day-to-day activities:
  • Assignment process
  • Performance evaluations
  • Promotion decisions
  • Termination decisions
