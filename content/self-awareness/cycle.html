How Can We Break The Cycle of Bias?
Stereotypes often affect our decisions and interactions with others, setting in motion a persistent cycle of bias.

How can we break this damaging cycle?

Be More Deliberate
By increasing our awareness of common stereotypes, we can be more deliberate about recognizing and questioning our stereotype-driven assumptions.
Seek and Use Factual and Relevant Information
Knowing that we are all susceptible to bias and stereotypes, seek and use real information on which to base our judgments and decisions.
