Microaggression Definitions
Microassault

Conscious and intentional verbal or nonverbal discriminatory actions, such as racial slurs (using the "N-word" or referring to someone as "Oriental"), name-calling (calling someone a "towel head"), avoidant behavior, or even racially charged symbols (swastikas/KKK hoods)

Microinsult

Subtle communications that transmit insensitivity or rudeness demeaning a person's heritage, background or identity ("He must be gay. Have you seen the way he dresses?")

Microinvalidation

Communications that subtly discredit the thoughts, feelings or experience of a person based on their heritage, background or identity. ("I prefer to see past race, gender, etc.; we're all just people.")
