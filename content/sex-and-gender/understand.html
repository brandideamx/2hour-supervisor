Sex
  • Being male, female, intersex - is determined by a person's biological sex: what physical body parts, chromosomes, and hormones they have.
Gender
  • Being a boy or a girl, a man or a woman - refers to a person's feeling of being a man, a woman, both, neither, or somewhere else along the gender spectrum.
While laws and policies often refer to "sex discrimination", many courts and organizations interpret sex discrimination to include discrimination based on gender such as gender identity and gender stereotypes.
