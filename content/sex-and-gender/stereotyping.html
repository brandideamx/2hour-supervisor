Sex Stereotyping
Sex stereotyping is another type of sex discrimination. It occurs when someone makes assumptions about a person or their abilities based on their sex.
Stereotypes may be based on:
  • Appearance
  • Behavior
  • Gender roles
  • Gender expression
  • Gender identity
Stereotypes about abilities may be based on:
  • Myth
  • Social expectation or generalization
