Strategies for Creating an Upstander Culture FOR ALLEN DEVELOPEMENT
You can act as an upstander in large and small ways everyday. Click the green arrows to reveal examples of upstander behaviors.

Recognize offensive conduct and interrupt it
  • Tell a colleague the term he/she used to describe someone is offensive
Publicly support someone who has been offended or neglected
  • Call attention to the efforts of a colleague who has been overlooked in a meeting or group email
Use body language to show disapproval
  • Turn away from someone telling an offensive joke
Help comfort hurt feelings
  • Approach someone you know has been offended and offer support
