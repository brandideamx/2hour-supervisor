Examples of Sexual Harassment
In a few minutes you will have an opportunity to practice identifying sexual harassment when you see it. Click on the green buttons below to see several examples. You'll be able to access this list during the activity if you need help.

Examples
  • Sexual comments, remarks or slurs
  • Sexual emails or text messages
  • Sexual teasing, jokes, remarks or questions
  • Showing another employee inappropriate websites with sexual content
  • Sexual remarks about a person's body, clothing or appearance
  • Patting, pinching, hugging, kissing or other unwanted touching
More Examples
  • Leering or ogling
  • Posting sexually explicit comments on social media sites
  • Pressure for dates
  • Inappropriate sexual advances
  • Solicitation of sexual favors or other sexually related behavior by promise of rewards
  • Coercion of sexual activity by threat or punishment
  • Sexual assault
