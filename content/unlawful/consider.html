Consider This:

You have three qualified candidates for an open position on your team. One of the candidates is a recent veteran. You decide to exclude him from your final round of consideration because you fear he may not have had enough time to readjust to civilian life.

Could this be considered harassment or discrimination?
  • Yes. This is a form of discrimination. Veteran status is a protected characteristic and it would be considered veteran status discrimination anytime being a veteran makes someone the target of workplace prejudice and employment discrimination.
