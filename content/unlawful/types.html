Discrimination and Harassment in the Workplace
Discrimination is differential treatment in hiring, promotion, termination, compensation or other terms, conditions or privileges of employment based on a protected characteristic. The following are examples of discrimination.

Discrimination Examples:
  • Not hiring due to age
  • Firing due to disability
  • Failing to promote due to gender
  • Reassigning to position with significantly different duties because of marital status
