Your Responsibilities as a Supervisor
As a supervisor you are responsible for enforcing the organization's policy against harassment and discrimination.

What can you do to enforce this policy?
  • Lead by example and always engage in appropriate conduct
  • If you witness or become aware of any harassment or discrimination, report it through the appropriate channels
  • If you receive a complaint of harassment or discrimination, even from someone other than the recipient of the harassment or discrimination, report it through the appropriate channels
  • Ensure that no retaliation takes place under your watch
