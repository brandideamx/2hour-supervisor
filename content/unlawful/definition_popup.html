Protected Category Information:
Protected Characteristics: Federal Law
  • Age (40 and older)
  • Citizenship
  • Color
  • Disability
  • Gender Identity
  • Genetic Information
  • National Origin
  • Pregnancy
  • Race
  • Religion
  • Sex
  • Sexual Orientation
  • Veteran/Military Status
Protected Characteristics: State Law

In addition to those characteristics protected under Federal law, the following characteristics are protected under State law:

  • Race, Color;
  • Ancestry, National Origin;
  • Religion, Creed;
  • Age (over 40);
  • Disability (Mental and/or Physical);
  • Sex, Gender (including pregnancy, childbirth, breastfeeding, or related medical conditions);
  • Sexual Orientation;
  • Gender Identity/Expression;
  • Medical Condition;
  • Genetic Information;
  • Marital Status;
  • Military/Veteran Status; and
  • Status as a Victim of Domestic Violence, Sexual Assault, or Stalking.
