Where Can Unlawful Harassment Occur? FOR ALLEN REDESIGN
This course focuses on "workplace" harassment.
But did you know that the workplace can be broadly defined and include more than just the building in which you work?
The workplace also includes any place employees are fulfilling their work responsibilities including any place an employee is in the course of business or during business-related or business sponsored functions.
Workplace Examples
  • Organization-sponsored parties or events
  • Off-site meetings, seminars, trainings, conferences
  • In the field
  • Organization-sponsored travel (car, airplane, train)
  • Restaurant, hotel, bar
  • Client's place of business
  • Vendor's place of business
  • Employees can be the target of harassment through calls, texts, email and social media
  • Employee interactions during non-work hours can also have an impact on the
    workplace and can be considered extensions of the work environment
