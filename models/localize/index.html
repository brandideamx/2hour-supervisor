How Do I Begin?

You are required to review the information in this course related to the state in which you are located. You are also required to review information for all of the states in which you manage employees.

If you work a portion of your time in New York State or you regularly interact with employees in New York City, you are required to be trained on NY laws.

If you regularly travel to IL for business or work remotely and interact regularly with IL employees, you are required to be trained on IL laws.
