Unconcious bias
Thanks for taking the time to do this exercise. It might have been a little uncomfortable, which is okay. Part of building self-awareness is understanding that all of us have what is called "unconscious bias" working behind the scenes.

We all have biases of one form or another due to our socialization, personal experiences, and values. Unconscious bias creeps into our everyday lives. Some common biases include:

Physically-based biases:
  • Obese people are lazy
  • Tall men are stronger, more decisive leaders
  • Disabled people require special attention
Gender-based biases:
  • Females are inferior in math and science
  • Women with children are less willing to travel for assignments
  • Men like to dominate a discussion
Age-based biases:
  • Older people are not as technologically savvy
  • Young employees have entitlement attitudes
